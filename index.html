About the project

This is my very straightforward and intuitive task manager project. Crafted with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this site helps you to organize your daily activities. Seamlessly(hopefuly) add, update, and mark tasks as complete, all within a clean and user-friendly interface.


How to use:

- To add new task you need to click on the icon in the top right corner. The prompts will guid your though the process


- To delete a task you need to click on the first icon in the bottom left corner of the task card.


- To mark a task as complete you need to click on the second icon in the bottom left corner of the task card.


- To mark a task as incomplete you need to click on the third icon in the bottom left corner of the task card.
